Frequently Asked Questions...

Can I register a property on behalf of someone else?

Since your house is a valuable asset, we need a registered phone number, Email ID, valid ownership proof & ID proof. If you have access to these documents for the property, you can list on someone's behalf.

Can I rent out the house by keeping a portion of the house locked?

Yes, it is possible for you to list a partially locked house. For instance, if you have a 3 BHK, and you want to keep 1 room locked, you can list it as a 2 BHK house on our platform and clarify the same in the House description.

Is posting property for selling/renting on RentMe FREE?

Yes, you can post your property listing online for sale or rent on RentMe for FREE. There are no charges involved. All buyer/tenant enquires will be shared with you completely free of cost.
